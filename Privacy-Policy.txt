GrowBet Bot Privacy Policy

At GrowBet, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our users' information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose data obtained through our Discord bot, GrowBet. By using GrowBet, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect:

1.1 User IDs: When interacting with GrowBet, we collect and store your Discord user ID. This information helps us provide personalized services and identify you within the Discord server.

1.2 Guild IDs: GrowBet may collect and store the IDs of the Discord servers (guilds) that you authorize it to join. This information allows us to provide server-specific features and functionality.

1.3 Discord User Names: We may collect and store your Discord user name for identification and communication purposes within the bot.

Use of Collected Information:

2.1 Personalization and Service Improvement: The data we collect, including user IDs and guild IDs, enables us to personalize and enhance your experience with GrowBet. It helps us understand usage patterns, identify trends, and improve our services.

2.2 Communication: We may use your Discord user name to communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide support related to GrowBet's functionality.

2.3 Statistical Analysis: We may aggregate and analyze the collected information to generate statistical insights. These insights do not personally identify any individual user and are used to improve the overall performance and features of GrowBet.

Data Security:

3.1 Data Protection: We implement security measures to protect the data collected through GrowBet, preventing unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of user information.

3.2 Data Retention: We retain collected data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

3.3 Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services to process and store data, ensuring their compliance with applicable privacy regulations.

Data Disclosure:

4.1 Authorized Access: We may share collected data with trusted employees, contractors, and service providers who require access to perform tasks on our behalf. These parties are bound by confidentiality obligations and must adhere to this Privacy Policy.

4.2 Legal Compliance: We may disclose user information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.

4.3 Change of Ownership: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify users of any such change and provide choices regarding their information.

Updating and Accessing Your Information:

5.1 Data Access and Correction: Users can request access, correction, or deletion of the personal information held about them by contacting us through the provided contact details.

5.2 Opting Out: Users can choose to opt out of certain data collection and communications by adjusting their settings within GrowBet or contacting us for assistance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

6.1 Policy Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of any material changes through GrowBet or other communication channels.
